We propose a hierarchical evaluation framework for autonomous driving systems using large language models (LLMs). The framework integrates multi-modal perception, chain-of-thought reasoning, and human evaluation to assess driving behavior across operational, tactical, and strategic levels. Experiments on CARLA Leaderboard demonstrate strong alignment with human judgment.
